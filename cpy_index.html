Four Steps. Hours, Not Months.

1

Collect: Independent Assessment

Every participant evaluates independently — no anchoring, no social pressure, identity masked. Generates ~200 scores and 10,000 words of expert reasoning. Everyone is heard.

2

Deliberate: Reason Ranking

Participants review anonymous samples of each other’s reasoning and vote what they find compelling. The best arguments rise on merit — not title, not volume.

3

Align: Interactive Q&A

Live sessions target the concerns surfaced in step 2. Evidence enters the shared record. AI agents contribute alongside humans. The group converges on what actually matters.

4

Act: Belief Updating

Participants rescore after new evidence. When people watch their own thinking evolve through a rigorous process, they own the outcome. Execution follows from genuine conviction.
