Knowledge Silos

Critical expertise remains trapped. Consulting firms charge $500K+ for insights already inside organizations.

The real problem is the challenge of converging on shared understanding.
The cost of coordination and alignment grows with the number of people involved.


Communication complexity exponentially scales. 20 people requires managing 190 lines of communication.

We use threaded conversations for deliberations and discussions today.
These methods do not help converge on what is of shared importance.

The unsolved and unpoken economic issue is coordination and alignement among human teams, human teams working with AI Agents and AI agents.

In order to create trust and ground AI models in collecitve intelligence of experts and human teams, we need to manage the process of everyone listing and reviewing each other's contributions.


An orchestrating AI Agent is needed that learns and guides deliberative conversations to a conclusive and shared understanding.

Human facilitators do this well but they too are limited by the complexity of the conversation and the number of people involved.

An AI orchestrator can do this by listening, learning and analyzing the conversation to guide the conversation to a conclusive and shared understanding.
